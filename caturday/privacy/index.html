Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device. That's it.

Last updated March 27, 2026

The short version

Caturday does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app works entirely on your device with no accounts, no tracking, and no analytics. We literally have no way to see your data.

What data does Caturday store?

Caturday stores a small amount of data locally on your device to make the app work:

  • Which cats you've revealed each day
  • Your favorite cats
  • Your app preferences

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, and we have no access to it.

Personal information

We do not collect any personal information. There are no accounts to create, no email addresses to enter, and no sign-in of any kind.

Analytics and tracking

Caturday does not include any analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not track how you use the app, and we do not use cookies.

Third-party services

Caturday does not connect to any third-party services. The app works entirely offline.

Children's privacy

Because we collect no data whatsoever, Caturday is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. But honestly, our approach is simple: we don't want your data. That's unlikely to change.

Questions about privacy? Reach out anytime.
